AN-Budget Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 13, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how ANBudget handles information. ANBudget is designed to be privacy-focused. Unless stated otherwise below, ANBudget does not collect, sell, or share your personal information.

Data You Enter

ANBudget stores the budgeting information you enter (for example: budgets, categories, transactions, wallet/bank labels) on your device. This information is used only to provide the app’s functionality.

Data Collection

ANBudget does not collect data from you.

  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party analytics SDKs
  • No tracking across apps or websites

Data Sharing

ANBudget does not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Cloud & Backups

If you use iOS device backups (such as iCloud backup), Apple may include app data as part of your device backup. This is controlled by your iOS settings and Apple’s services, not by AN-Budget.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information stored by the app. No method of storage is 100% secure, but ANBudget is built to minimize exposure by keeping data on-device.

Children’s Privacy

ANBudget is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: eui.h.an@outlook.com

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page.
